==================
MapReduce Service
==================

MapReduce Service (MRS) provides a reliable, secure, and easy-to-use operation and maintenance platform for storing and analyzing large volumes of data. With this platform, you can independently apply for and use the Hadoop or Spark hosting service to create Hadoop or Spark clusters, as well as batch analyze and compute large volumes of non-time-critical data.
